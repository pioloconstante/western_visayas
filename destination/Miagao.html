Miagao Church (Iloilo)

Miagao Church is a historic masterpiece and a UNESCO World Heritage Site that showcases the rich cultural and religious heritage of the Philippines. Built in the late 18th century, it features a unique blend of Baroque and local Filipino architectural styles. The church’s detailed façade tells biblical stories carved in stone, making it not only a place of worship but also a cultural treasure. Surrounded by a peaceful town atmosphere, visitors can appreciate its centuries-old history, strong stone structure, and artistic beauty that has stood the test of time.
