Campuestohan Highland Resort (Negros Occidental)

Campuestohan Highland Resort is a family-friendly mountain resort offering cool weather and exciting attractions. It features giant statues, swimming pools, zipline adventures, and fun activities for all ages. The resort provides panoramic views of mountains and city lights, making it both relaxing and entertaining. It is a perfect destination for bonding, photography, and weekend getaways.
