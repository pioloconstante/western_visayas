Islas de Gigantes (Iloilo)

Islas de Gigantes is a hidden paradise composed of stunning islands surrounded by clear blue waters, dramatic limestone cliffs, and untouched natural beauty. This destination is perfect for travelers who love adventure and exploration. Island hopping here reveals breathtaking sandbars that disappear and appear with the tide, mysterious caves waiting to be explored, and pristine beaches that feel untouched by time. Fresh seafood is also a highlight, especially the famous scallops that the islands are known for. Islas de Gigantes offers a peaceful escape away from crowded tourist spots, making it a dream destination for nature lovers.
