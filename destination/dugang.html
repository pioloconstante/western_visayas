Bugang–Malumpati River (Antique)

Bugang–Malumpati River is one of the cleanest inland waters in the Philippines, known for its striking blue-green color and refreshing temperature. Surrounded by lush forests, it offers a peaceful escape into nature where visitors can swim, float, or enjoy bamboo rafting along its calm flow. The area is well-preserved, making it a perfect destination for eco-tourism and relaxation. It is a place where nature feels untouched and pure.
