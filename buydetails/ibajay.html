IBAJAY RICE COFFEE (KAPENG BIGAS)

Rice Coffee, locally known as Kapeng Bigas, is a traditional beverage from Ibajay, Aklan made by roasting rice grains until they turn dark brown and aromatic, then brewing them like coffee. It has a mild, toasty flavor and is often enjoyed as a caffeine-free alternative to regular coffee.
